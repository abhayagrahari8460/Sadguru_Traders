Our Infrastructure

Land Area & Warehouse

Land Area: 2 acres of strategically located land in Ramnagar, Bihar, providing ample space for operations, storage, and future expansion. The land is well-connected to major transportation routes for efficient logistics.

Warehouse Construction: 5,000 sq.ft modern warehouse facility designed for optimal storage conditions, with proper ventilation, moisture control, and organized storage systems. The warehouse can accommodate large inventories while maintaining product quality.

Office Space & Loading Areas

Office Space: 1,000 sq.ft dedicated office space for administrative operations, customer service, and management activities. The office is equipped with modern facilities for efficient business operations.

Loading/Unloading Area: 1,500 sq.ft dedicated loading and unloading area with proper access for trucks and heavy vehicles. This area is designed for quick and efficient material handling operations.

Utilities & Safety Measures

Ventilation: Advanced ventilation systems throughout the warehouse to maintain optimal storage conditions and prevent moisture-related damage to cement and other materials.

Fire Safety: Comprehensive fire safety measures including fire extinguishers, emergency exits, and safety protocols to ensure secure operations and protect valuable inventory.

Security: 24/7 security systems with CCTV monitoring and trained security personnel to protect inventory and facilities.
